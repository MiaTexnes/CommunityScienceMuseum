Admission

Free admission We offer guided tours with fee of 30kr per person We recommend contacting us for visits with groups over 20 people

Groups

We welcome all groups, but we kindly request that you contact us approximately one week prior to your visit if you have more than 20 people, due to fire regulations.

Accessibility

The museum is committed to inclusivity, providing accessibility for diverse needs with wheelchair ramps, elevators, audio guides, tactile displays, captioned videos, sign language interpretation, and assistive listening devices are available free of charge.

Shop/cafe

The museum's shop/café offers a variety of easily accessible food options and curated souvenirs. Visitors can enjoy meals, snacks, and find unique mementos to remember their museum experience, including artwork reproductions and locally crafted items. We have seating areas where you are free to enjoy both brought food and food bought in the cafe

In case of emergency

Visitor safety is paramount at the museum. We have well-marked fire exits, vigilant guards, and a state-of-the-art alarm system for swift response in case of emergencies. Regular fire drills ensure preparedness and familiarization with evacuation procedures. Your safety and enjoyment are our top priorities.
