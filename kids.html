Where learning becomes fun

Kids corner

Welcome to our interactive science museum's Kids Corner, a wonderland of exploration and creativity for young minds! Here, children can dive into a world of hands-on activities and imaginative play that sparks their curiosity and fuels their love for science and technology.

In our Kids Corner, budding engineers can design and construct their very own rockets, experimenting with different materials and propulsion systems to reach for the stars. Future programmers can delve into the world of robotics, coding and controlling robots to perform exciting tasks and solve challenges.

For the young artists, we offer a drawing station where they can unleash their creativity and illustrate their scientific discoveries. Our collection of captivating and educational books invites children to dive into exciting adventures, scientific concepts, and the wonders of the natural world.

But the excitement doesn't stop there! In our Kids Corner, children can step into the shoes of robots and engage in pretend play, exploring the possibilities of artificial intelligence and robotics. They can experience what it's like to think, move, and communicate like their mechanical counterparts.

Our Kids Corner is a place where learning meets fun, where children can engage their minds, ignite their imaginations, and cultivate a passion for science and technology. Join us in this interactive space, where rockets soar, robots come to life, drawings tell stories, and young minds are inspired to reach for the stars!
